WELCOME FAMILY BENEFICIARIES

Each of you is an equal Beneficiary of the combined assets of Grandmom & Poppy — which includes the house & its contents.

The purpose of this website is to provide a structure to distribute any house contents that Beneficiaries might want prior to selling the house.

PROCEDURE

  1. Please review this House Contents Inventory. A search bar is at the top. Clicking a photo opens a new browser window with an enlarged photo.

    • Items identified as *Sells with House are not available for distribution to Beneficiaries herein — Beneficiaries will receive their value through the increased value of the house "For Sale" package.

  2. Send me an eMail with the items you wish to possess. Please include the item name & number.

    • DEADLINE for CONTENTS CHOICES is: Saturday FEB 17, 2024 at 11:59PM
      This concentrated time frame helps to expedite the next phases of the house selling process: appraisal, price setting, etc. My goal is to sell the house as soon as possible to reduce its exposure to the next Florida hurricane season.

  3. Once I hear from everyone regarding which, if any, items you wish to possess, I will update the House Contents Inventory web site showing who would like to have which items.

  4. Items not chosen by any Beneficiary — will either be sold with the house OR sold to a "collectibles" merchant for cash that then pours back into the estate for equal division among the 8 Beneficiaries.

  5. Items chosen by only one Beneficiary — will be granted & given to that Beneficiary free — as would be the wish of Grandmom and Poppy.

  6. Items chosen by multiple Beneficiaries — each Beneficiary wanting the item will submit a private email bid (to me) for the item as follows:

    • PLACE a ONE-TIME OFFER — Multiple Beneficiaries wishing a specific item can choose to 'bid' for the item with a ONE-TIME PRICE OFFER. Highest PRICE OFFER 'wins' the item. Once I receive all the private email bids, I will update the web site to indicate who was the highest bidder & the value of the winning bid. Each Beneficiary with 'winning' bids will then write a check to me (Maryann) to pay for the items.

    • Once all the checks for winning bids are in, I will distribute the proceeds equally to all Beneficiaries. Thus, this process benefits all of us.

  7. Some Beneficiaries may wish none or only a few household items, while other Beneficiaries may wish quite a few. Fair distribution does not mean that we each receive the exact number of items but that we are happy for other Beneficiaries to have items that we do not wish ourselves. Fairness is what we collectively agree for it to be — not confined merely to mathematical equivalency.

  8. Kenster will mail smaller items to you at his expense. Larger items would need to be picked up from the house.
